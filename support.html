Frequently asked

How do I play?
Each card is a true-or-false statement with a 5-second timer. Swipe right for YES, left for NO. Answer fast for bonus points and build a combo.
What's the Daily Challenge?
A shared set of 25 questions every day. Complete it to grow your day-streak — miss a day and the streak resets.
Why do I see ads?
Ads keep QuizPliz free. Watching a short rewarded ad can revive a run. On iOS you choose whether ads are personalized via the tracking prompt.
What data do you collect?
Your scores and progress stay on your device. Diagnostics and ad/analytics data are handled by Google (AdMob, Firebase). See our Privacy Policy.
Which languages are supported?
English and Spanish. Tap the language pills on the home screen to switch.
How do I delete my data?
There's no account — uninstalling the app removes all on-device data. You can also reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.
